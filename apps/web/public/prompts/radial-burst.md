Begin with the composition empty, then send the elements inward from every edge of the frame as if pulled toward their final positions by a central force.

Determine each element's entrance direction from the nearest canvas edge. Elements should travel inward quickly, scale from slightly smaller to full size, and rotate by a few degrees in the direction of travel before straightening. Start the outermost elements first and stagger the rest toward the center.

The motion should feel energetic but controlled. Avoid simultaneous arrival: create a rapid sequence of distinct impacts, followed by a brief final hold on the completed artwork.
