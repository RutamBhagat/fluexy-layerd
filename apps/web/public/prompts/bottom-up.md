Assemble the artwork from bottom to top. Start with the lowest elements and work upward in clear horizontal bands.

Each element rises from below its final position while fading in. Give larger elements a slower, weightier entrance and smaller elements a quicker response. Add a restrained upward overshoot before they settle back into place.

Use a short stagger within each band and a more noticeable pause between bands. Avoid having the whole composition move at once, and finish with every element at its original position and scale.
